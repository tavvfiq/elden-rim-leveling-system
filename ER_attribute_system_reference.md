# Attribute System & scaling

This document defines the core attributes and their specific contributions to player statistics, defensive layers, and status thresholds.

## 1. Core Attributes

### 1.1 Vigor (VIG)
Primarily governs survivability and physical resilience.
* **HP Growth:** Primary source of Max HP. 
    * *Soft Caps:* 40 / 60.
* **Fire Resistance:** Provides a significant boost to Fire Defense (Layer 1).
* **Immunity:** Increases resistance to **Poison** and **Rot** status effects (Section 4.4, 4.5).

### 1.2 Mind (MND)
Governs mental energy and focus.
* **MP Growth:** Primary source of Max Magicka.
    * *Soft Caps:* 50 / 60.
* **Focus:** Increases resistance to **Sleep** (Section 4.6) and Madness.
* **Magical Defense:** Provides a minor boost to Layer 1 Magic Defense.

### 1.3 Endurance (END)
Governs physical activity and equipment capacity.
* **Stamina Growth:** Primary source of Max Stamina.
    * *Soft Caps:* 50.
* **Equip Load:** Increases the weight threshold before movement penalties apply.
* **Robustness:** Increases resistance to **Hemorrhage** (Section 4.2) and **Frost** (Section 4.3).
* **Physical Defense:** Provides a minor boost to Layer 1 Physical Defense.

### 1.4 Strength (STR)
Governs the ability to wield heavy armaments and heavy armor.
* **Physical Defense:** The primary attribute for Layer 1 Physical Defense (Section 2.1).
* **Weapon Scaling:** Increases damage for "Heavy" infused weapons and Strike-type weapons.
* **Two-Handing Bonus:** While two-handing a weapon, the effective STR value is multiplied by 1.5x.

### 1.5 Dexterity (DEX)
Governs finesse, casting speed, and balance.
* **Casting Speed:** Shortens the animation time for spells.
* **Fall Damage:** Reduces HP lost from non-lethal falls.
* **Stance/Poise:** Provides minor resistance against being staggered.
* **Weapon Scaling:** Increases damage for "Keen" infused weapons and Slash/Pierce-type weapons.

### 1.6 Intelligence (INT)
Governs the power of Sorceries and Glintstone magic.
* **Magic Defense:** The primary attribute for Layer 1 Magic Defense (Section 2.1).
* **Weapon Scaling:** Increases damage for Magic and Cold weapon infusions.
* **Spell Requirement:** High-tier spells require specific INT thresholds to cast.

### 1.7 Faith (FTH)
Governs the power of Incantations and holy arts.
* **Elemental Defense:** Boosts Layer 1 Fire, Lightning, and Holy defenses.
* **Weapon Scaling:** Increases damage for Fire and Sacred weapon infusions.
* **Spell Requirement:** High-tier incantations require specific FTH thresholds.

### 1.8 Arcane (ARC)
Governs discovery and latent power.
* **Discovery:** Increases the drop rate of items from fallen enemies.
* **Status Scaling:** Increases the buildup rate of **Hemorrhage**, **Poison**, and **Rot** (if the weapon scales with Arcane).
* **Holy Defense:** Provides a significant boost to Holy/Vitality resistance.
